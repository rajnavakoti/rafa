# FAQ

## I would like to start exploring the developer portal, what could be a good entry point?

Here are a few directions for how to get started.

- For managing your software, a good start could be to register your entities to the software catalog, such as APIs or backend services, see [software catalog](catalog/overview.md), and make use of software catalog functions and enabled open source plugins. By starting to populate the software catalog, it also enables others to explore and discover services in the INGKA eco-system.

- For managing your technical documentation, both for smooth team internal and external access, see [techdocs documentation](techdocs/overview.md) how to get your documentation published and accessible in the Developer portal.

## I miss platform capabilities in the portal, e.g. managing infrastructure and security capabilities, where are they?

The Developer portal is still in its early days, with a limited set of workflows in place as of today. Plugin development and co-creation initiatives are running, and we expect to soon see results of that work.

See [roadmap](https://github.com/ingka-group-digital/developer-portal/projects/3) for active and planned workflows.

If you are missing anything, don't assume it is planned or acted upon, and get involved in the project, see [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/ingka-group-digital/developer-portal/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) how to get started and to make a difference.

## I found a potential bug, how do I report it?

You can [report a bug here](https://github.com/ingka-group-digital/developer-portal/issues/new?assignees=&labels=bug&template=bug_template.md)

You can also try to make a contribution by fixing it and proposing a fix for it. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/ingka-group-digital/developer-portal/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).

## What support is there for the portal and what is the SLA?

Technology Service Area Engineering Services is responsible for the operations of the developer portal.

Support hours for the Developer portal is currently "SL2".

| Type                   |                    |
| ---------------------- | ------------------ |
| Availability           | `90%`              |
| Service hours (H \* D) | `24 * 5`           |
| Support hours (H \* D) | `(8-17) * 5 (CET)` |
